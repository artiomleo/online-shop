Areas of application
Hygienic and surgical hand disinfection.
Notes on use
desmanol® is used undiluted. During the contact time for
hygienic and surgical hand disinfection, doses of desmanol®
should be applied in such a way that the hands remain well
moistened over the entire application period.
Microbiological efficacy
desmanol® is:
• bactericidal (incl. TB bacilli) • fungicidal • virucidal
